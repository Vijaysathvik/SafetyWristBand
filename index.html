Welcome to the Safety Wristband

The Safety Wristband is designed to keep you safe in any situation. Whether you're walking alone at night or in an emergency, this wristband ensures that your location is shared with loved ones and produces a loud sound to deter potential threats.

Features

  • Live Location Sharing: The wristband shares your live location with predefined contacts within seconds.
  • Loud Sound Production: Press the button to produce a loud sound that can attract attention and deter attackers.
  • Emergency Alert: Send an instant alert to your loved ones with just one press.
  • Alert to Nearest Police station:The nearest police will be notified within seconds

Why should you use our Wrist Band

Here's the reason why you should use our product:

  • Immediate Assistance: In an emergency, the wristband sounds a loud buzzer, signaling for nearby help while also alerting pre-saved contacts.
  • Real-Time Location Tracking: Accurate, live GPS tracking allows friends and family to see exactly where you are, providing reassurance and enabling faster assistance.
  • Automatic Police Assistance: The wristband can identify the nearest police station, giving you immediate access to help. This feature could be lifesaving in high-risk situations.
  • Simple, Reliable Design: With a preloaded SIM card, the device works independently, requiring no complex setup or smartphone pairing.
