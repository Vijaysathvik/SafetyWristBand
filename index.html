Welcome to the Safety Wristband

The Safety Wristband is designed to keep you safe in any situation. Whether you're walking alone at night or in an emergency, this wristband ensures that your location is shared with loved ones and produces a loud sound to deter potential threats.

Features

  • Live Location Sharing: The wristband shares your live location with predefined contacts via GPS.
  • Loud Sound Production: Press the button to produce a loud sound that can attract attention and deter attackers.
  • Emergency Alert: Send an instant alert to your loved ones with just one press.

How It Works

The Safety Wristband is equipped with a GPS module and a speaker. When the button on the wristband is pressed:

  • The wristband sends your live location to your predefined contacts via a mobile app.
  • Simultaneously, it produces a loud sound to alert those nearby and deter potential threats.
  • The wristband is lightweight, waterproof,and designed for everyday wear.
